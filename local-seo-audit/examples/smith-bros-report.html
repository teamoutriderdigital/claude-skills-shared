The 3 Biggest Issues Found

1. Three Duplicate GBP Listings CRITICAL

Smith Bros has THREE separate Google Business Profile listings splitting 545 reviews and confusing Google:

  • Listing A (Storefront): 233 reviews, has physical address, active Google Posts — this is the well-managed primary listing that appears in local pack
  • Listing B (Service Area): 275 reviews, NO physical address, also active — but cannot appear in local pack because it has no address
  • Listing C (Abandoned): 37 reviews, NO physical address, 3-4 year old reviews — dead weight

The three-way review split is the core problem. 545 reviews on ONE listing would make Smith Bros the highest-reviewed mobile detailer in North County.

2. Duplicate Listings Diluting Authority HIGH

While Listing A is well-optimized with secondary categories, business description, and services populated, the existence of two additional duplicate listings (B and C) is splitting 545 reviews three ways and may be confusing Google's algorithms about which listing to surface in local pack results.

3. No Website Schema Markup HIGH

The website has zero structured data (JSON-LD). Competitors have comprehensive LocalBusiness, AggregateRating, FAQPage, and Service schemas that help them appear in rich results.

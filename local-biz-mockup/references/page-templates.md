# Page Section Templates

Blueprints for every page type. Each section includes purpose, layout, and content requirements.

---

## Home Page

### Hero
- **Layout**: Full-width background image with gradient overlay. Left-aligned text on desktop, centered on mobile.
- **Content**: h1 headline (5-8 words max), subtitle (1-2 sentences), trust badges (license, certifications), primary CTA button, secondary CTA button.
- **Image**: Best landscape photo of the business at work. Must be high-contrast-friendly with overlay.

### Stats Bar
- **Layout**: Dark background, 4 stats in a row with dividers.
- **Content**: 4 key proof points — years experience, projects completed, key metric (% focus, rating), license/credential.

### About / Who We Are
- **Layout**: 2-column — text left, image right on desktop. Stacked on mobile.
- **Content**: Company overview (2 short paragraphs), key stat callout box, value proposition checklist (5 items with checkmarks), CTA link.
- **Image**: Project photo or team photo.

### Services Grid
- **Layout**: Section heading + 3-column card grid (6 cards typical). Centered CTA button below.
- **Content**: Per card — SVG icon, service title, 1-2 sentence description. "View All Services" CTA linking to /services.

### Industries / Sectors
- **Layout**: Section heading + 3-column or 6-column grid of sector cards.
- **Content**: Per card — sector name, 1-line description of project types in that sector.

### Social Proof / Why Choose Us
- **Layout**: Dark background. 3-column feature blocks centered.
- **Content**: Per block — SVG icon in circle, headline, 1-2 sentence description. Focus on: reliability, clean record, crew quality.

### CTA Banner
- **Layout**: Brand-primary background, centered text, 2 buttons.
- **Content**: Short headline ("Ready to Build?"), 1-line subtitle, phone CTA + quote request CTA.

## Services Page

### Hero (compact)
- **Layout**: Shorter hero (py-24 to py-32). Centered text.
- **Content**: h1 headline, subtitle about service range.

### Commercial/Primary Services
- **Layout**: Left-aligned heading + description paragraph, then 3-column card grid.
- **Content**: 5-7 service cards with detailed descriptions (2-3 sentences each). SVG icons. `large` card variant.

### Photo Break
- **Layout**: Full-width image with dark overlay and centered quote text.
- **Content**: One punchy tagline. Best project photo.

### Residential/Secondary Services
- **Layout**: Same as primary services but on light-bg section.
- **Content**: 4-6 service cards.

### Market Sectors
- **Layout**: 3-column grid of sector cards with descriptions.
- **Content**: 6 sectors with 1-line descriptions each.

### CTA Banner
- Same as homepage.

## Contact Page

### Hero (compact)
- **Layout**: Shorter hero, centered text.
- **Content**: h1 "Start Your Project" style headline, subtitle about response time.

### Contact Content
- **Layout**: 2-column — form (60%) left, contact info (40%) right. Stacks on mobile.
- **Form fields**: Name*, Company, Email*, Phone*, Project Type (select), Budget Range (select), Project Details (textarea). Submit button with icon.
- **Info column**: Phone (primary + estimates), Email, WhatsApp, Address, Business Hours, Credential badges.

### Map Section
- **Layout**: Full-width Google Maps embed in rounded container.
- **Content**: Heading "Our Location" + service area description.

### CTA Banner
- **Layout**: Brand-primary background, phone CTA prominent.
